        How to play

        Rules

        Place tiles on the open grid to make connected words. Words read left-to-right or top-to-bottom, and every placed tile must belong to a valid word.

        Each player has their own board and rack, while everyone draws from the same bag. Finish a valid rack to peel one tile for every player, or dump one tile to draw up to three replacements.

        When the bag is empty, the first player to place every tile in valid words wins.
